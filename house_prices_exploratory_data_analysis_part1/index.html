House Prices - Exploratory Data Analysis Part 1

This post covers the Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of the famous competition House Prices: Advanced Regression Techniques from Kaggle

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) is the most important step of any Data Science problem. It is often the first step in data analysis before implementing any statistical technique and the most time consuming Job but undersanding the data and making sense out of it can save you a lot of time while Data Cleaning, Feaure Engineering and improving model's accuracy.

The purpose of exploratory data analysis is to:

  • Understand the data and try to make sense out of it.
